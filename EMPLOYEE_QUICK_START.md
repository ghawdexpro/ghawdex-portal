## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
**A:** Contact your admin (usually admin@ghawdex.com) and ask for a password reset. They'll send you a temporary password.

### Q: Can I edit the company information?
**A:** Only administrators and managers can edit content. Regular employees can view only.

### Q: How do I create a new calendar event?
**A:** Calendar events are created by managers and admins through the admin panel. Contact your manager to request an event.

### Q: Can I add a document to the wiki?
**A:** Depends on your role. Managers and admins can add wiki pages. Ask your manager if you need to contribute documentation.

### Q: How often is the content updated?
**A:** Content is updated by managers and admins as needed. Check back regularly for updates.

### Q: Can I access the portal from my phone?
**A:** Yes! The portal works on mobile devices. Just visit https://portal.ghawdex.pro in your mobile browser.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes! The portal uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/SSL). Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text.

### Q: What if I find a bug or have feedback?
**A:** Contact your admin or IT department. They can forward feedback to the development team.
